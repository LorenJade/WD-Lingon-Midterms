About Me

Loren Jade Lingon

Loren Jade F. Lingon is a full-time student taking Bachelor of Multimedia Arts at FEU Institute of Technology. She is an experienced artist and self-taught video editor who can produce visually appealing and striking compositions, while exploring the world beyond arts to improve her skills and capabilities.

Cartoons have always piqued her curiosity, and as she progressed in her artistic development, she found herself adapting the artwork as she goes along and try new things along the journey ahead.
